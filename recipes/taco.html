Taco

Description:

This is a recipe to make some delicious tacos. Not only are they healthy but the ingredients are easy to find on any food market, so feel free to follow this recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla
  • Meat
  • onion
  • cilantro
  • lime
  • red or green sauce

Steps:

  1. heat tortillas
  2. Cook meat
  3. Wash onions and cilantro and slice them up
  4. put everything on the tortilla and close it
  5. Add lime
  6. Add either sauce
  7. Enjoy!
