Burger

Description:

This is a recipe to make a delicious burger. Not only is it healthy but the ingredients are easy to find on any food market, so feel free to follow this recipe.

Ingredients:

  • Burger bread
  • Meat
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Any sauce you want

Steps:

  1. Toast bread
  2. Cook meat
  3. Wash tomatoes and lettuce, then slice them
  4. Stack the ingredients on the bread
  5. Add sauce
  6. Enjoy!
